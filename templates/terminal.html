About Our Terminals

At Global, we operate a vast network of 42 terminals, making us a leading provider in the logistics industry. With our extensive terminal infrastructure, we have the capacity to handle a total of 25,704,734 units, demonstrating our ability to efficiently manage large-scale operations.


Our terminals are strategically located and designed to handle a wide range of products. We have dedicated 42 terminals for general cargo, 46 for chemicals, 17 for vegoils, and 14 for biofuels, allowing us to cater to the diverse needs of our clients across various sectors. Whether it's transporting goods, chemicals, or specialized products, our terminals are equipped to handle it all.

When it comes to safety and security, we prioritize the utmost care for your products. Our state-of-the-art equipment and cutting-edge technologies ensure that your goods are handled with the highest level of precision and protection. We take pride in our robust infrastructure and innovative approach, which enable us to maintain the integrity and quality of your products throughout the logistics process.


At Global, we understand that each client has unique needs. That's why we strive to provide personalized services that align with their specific requirements. Our customer-centric approach, combined with our unwavering commitment to maintaining the highest standards of service, has earned us the trust of businesses across various sectors. When you partner with Global, you can expect tailored solutions and exceptional service that exceed your expectations.
